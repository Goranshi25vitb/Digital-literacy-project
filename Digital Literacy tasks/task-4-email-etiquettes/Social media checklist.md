# Social Media Checklist

## DO'S

#### 1. Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.
#### 2. Use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings regularly.
#### 3. Check facts before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
#### 4. Treat others politely and avoid engaging in online conflicts.
#### 5. Balance social media use with studies and real-life activities.

## DON'Ts

#### 1. Avoid posting phone numbers, address, or financial details.
#### 2. Never post harmful, offensive, or abusive comments.
#### 3. Don’t trust unknown links or messages
#### 4. Avoid real-time location posts that can risk your safety.
#### 5. Emotional posts can harm your reputation and relationships.
