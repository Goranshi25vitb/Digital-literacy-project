# Preventions

## Preventions: 
### Stay safe online checklist: 
##### 1. Make super-strong passwords for every account—no "123456" or your 
##### birthday! Mix uppercase/lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols, like 
##### "BhopalR0cks!2026". And never reuse the same one everywhere. 
##### 2.Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) everywhere important—email, 
##### Facebook, your bank app. It's that extra code sent to your phone that 
##### makes hackers sweat. 
##### 3. Skip clicking links in random emails, texts, or posts, even if it "looks" like 
##### from your bank. Type the web address yourself instead. 
##### 4. Steer clear of public Wi-Fi (cafes, trains) for banking or logins—it's like 
##### shouting your secrets in a crowded market. 
##### 5. Only grab apps from Google Play or App Store. Skip those shady APK 
##### downloads from weird sites. 
##### 6. Think twice before posting personal stuff on social media—your address 
##### or phone number is gold to scammers. 
##### 7. Never share your OTP, password, or details, no matter who calls claiming 
##### "bank emergency." Real companies won't ask. 
##### 8. Before typing passwords or card details, double-check the site: Does it 
start with "https://" and have the right name (like sbi.co.in, not 
sb1.co.in)? 


### UPI Tips: 
##### 1. Never enter your UPI pin to receive money 
##### 2. Never share OTP on the phone or with anyone
